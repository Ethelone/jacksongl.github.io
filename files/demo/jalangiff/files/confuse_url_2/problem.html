Confusing URL II

Again, what would happen if you click the following link or copy and paste it into your browser's address bar? Without careful inspection, some casual readers might think the following URL jumps to www.google.com, but again Bing is waiting for them.

The character behind www.google.com is just a character that looks like a normal slash in URL (Actually it is fraction slash. BTW division slash can also do the trick.). Therefore, the browser engine will interpret as a sub-domain of bing.com.
